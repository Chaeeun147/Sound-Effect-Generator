# Sound-Effect-Generator
Have you ever been in a situation where you thought you needed a sound effect to fill the void of silence? If yes, then this is the perfect device for you!

This is a C4-inspired sound effect player. When you are in an awkward situation that requires a sound effect to fill the void, or when you wish you could just play a meme sound effect, this device would be perfect for you!!!!

Carry this device around, and when you need a sound effect, swipe the card to play it!
